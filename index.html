Confloose

A confloose (or "loose configuration") is a collection of the worst a startup script can do for you.

When someone leaves his computer without locking it, the tradition here is to install such scripts on his computer while he's away -- as such, a confloose is a challenge (it makes you learn your unix environment the hard way), and a way to remind you to never leave your computer and/or data in public space without protection.

Rules :

  1. Don't destroy user data.
  2. Don't steal user data.
  3. Be fun and creative.
